About Us

We provide personalized care for addiction recovery. The concept is to create an affordable addiction management program that integrates both online and offline support to help individuals overcome addiction. The online component features a mobile app offering personalized recovery plans, virtual support groups, educational resources, and progress tracking, accessible at any time and from anywhere. The offline component provides face-to-face interaction through local support groups, counseling sessions, workshops, and community events, which foster personal connections and hands-on learning. Our vision is to make addiction recovery support accessible and cost-effective for everyone, reducing addiction rates by providing comprehensive, low-cost resources that combine digital convenience with local, community-based support, ultimately helping individuals lead healthier, addiction-free lives..
